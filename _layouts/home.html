About Me

Hi there, I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am grateful to have prof. Frederic Sala and prof. Ramya K. Vinayak as my advisors. My research interests are in machine learning theory, particularly in settings where labeled data are scarce and noisy. Before coming to Madison I worked at IBM Research India, and spent two wonderful years at IISc Bangalore to obtain my masters in CS.

Research Interests

— Weak Supervision, Active Learning, Auto Labeling.
— non-Euclidean ML.
— Understanding deep learning.
— Meaning of life :)
